Our Mission

Who is The Phlebotomy Trainer?

During a gap year from university, I entered the NHS seeking real-world experience. After being trained as a phlebotomist in late 2011, I began working at a local General Practice surgery. My training didn't stop there; I transitioned to a major NHS hospital trust in London in early 2013, working across various departments—from A&E to outpatient clinics—gaining unparalleled experience across all hospital floors.

Having successfully trained numerous phlebotomy students within the clinical environment, I realized the theory-practice gap was huge. That's why I started bridging the gap with real clinical practice, and today run The Phlebotomy Trainer training others to overcome their fears and begin a career in Phlebotomy.
